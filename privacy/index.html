Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Sheda ("Sheda", "we", "us") operates a marketplace that connects guests with property owners ("hosts") for short-term stays in Iraq and the Kurdistan Region. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices. By using the Sheda app, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

Account information

  • Name, email address, phone number, and the city you live in.
  • Your role (guest or host) and profile photo, if you add one.

Identity verification

To keep the community safe, guests and hosts verify their identity. You provide a photo of a government ID and a selfie. These images are analyzed by an automated identity-verification service (Google Gemini) to confirm the ID appears genuine and matches the selfie. We store these documents securely in a private, access-controlled bucket. They are visible only to you, to authorized Sheda administrators, and — for a confirmed booking — to the host you are staying with, so they know who is staying at their property.

Listings, location, and media

  • Hosts provide property details, photos and videos, a text address, and a map location (latitude/longitude). With your permission, we may use your device location to help you place a property pin.
  • Photos and videos you upload are stored in our media storage, organized per property.

Bookings and payments

  • Booking dates, guest count, and amounts.
  • Payments are processed by our third-party payment provider, Wayl. Your card or banking details are entered on Wayl's secure checkout and are never stored on Sheda's servers. We retain a record of the transaction (amount, status, and a reference/order ID) to operate the booking and pay out hosts.

Messages and reviews

  • Messages you exchange with other users, including any photos or videos you send in chat.
  • Reviews and ratings you leave after a stay.

Device and usage

  • A device push-notification token (if you enable notifications) so we can alert you about bookings, payments, messages, and verification updates.
  • Basic technical data needed to run and secure the app.

2. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account and listings.
  • To verify identity and reduce fraud and abuse.
  • To process bookings, payments, and host payouts.
  • To enable messaging, reviews, and notifications.
  • To provide customer support and comply with legal obligations.
Payments are for real-world stays, not digital content. Sheda facilitates physical accommodation between independent hosts and guests. Payment is collected through Wayl for a real-world service (a place to stay) and is not a purchase of digital goods or in-app content.

3. How we share information

  • Between guests and hosts as needed to complete a booking (e.g. the host sees the guest's verified identity for a confirmed stay; both parties see each other's public profile and can message).
  • Service providers who help us operate: our backend and storage provider (Supabase), our identity-verification provider (Google Gemini), our payment provider (Wayl), and push-notification delivery (Expo). They process data only to provide their service.
  • Legal: when required by law, or to protect rights, safety, and the integrity of the platform.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service, resolve disputes, and meet legal and financial record-keeping requirements. Payout and payment records are retained for accounting and audit purposes.

5. Security

Identity documents and payment proofs are kept in private, access-controlled storage. Access to data is governed by row-level security and role-based permissions. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

6. Your choices and rights

  • View and edit your profile, including your city, in the app.
  • Turn notifications on or off in your device settings.
  • Delete your account and all associated data directly in the app (Profile → Delete account), or by contacting us. See our Data Deletion page for details.
  • Request access to or correction of your personal data by contacting us.

7. Children

Sheda is intended for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
